Chicago Bears Beer Club
9:00PM MARCH 6TH, 2015 | BUFFALO WILD WINGS
Clemence Bridgewater
Moderator since June, 2012

This is a Beer Club for anyone who is interested the Chicago Bears. Whether you are only a fan because you’re from Chicago, your buddies are Bears fans and you cheer with them, you’ve been a die-hard fan since you were 4, or you’re a hardcore sports statistics enthusiast, this Beer Club will have something to offer you. Request to attend this Beer Club and you’re signing up for a solid block of your near future guaranteed to be entirely concerned with all things Bears (and beer). Bring your knowledge, competitive spirit, fandom, and of course a six-pack to the table and meet some new Bears fans.
