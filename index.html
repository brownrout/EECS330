Whatever your angle, Beer Club has something for you

Beer Club is a new way for people to come together, hang out, and have meaningful interactions. Whether it’s meeting with fellow alumni, watching the game with some fans, or simply casually chatting with total strangers, Beer Club has something for you. Unlike other meetup services, Beer Club strives to foster conversation. We take much pride in our team of highly qualified moderators that accompany clubs and ensure the Beer Club experience is consistently excellent.

How Beer Club Works

Want to start a beer club of your own?
Apply to become a moderator.

Becoming a moderator means that you are passionate about people, beer, and networking. Once you submit your information to us, we will review your profile and set up a quick phone call to see if you’re right for the job. It’s not a hard one, but a moderator needs to be able to motivate conversation throughout a Beer Club session and make sure that conversation remains somewhat related to the topic at hand. You’ll still get to relax and enjoy the normal perks of Beer Club, but with a little added responsibility. You’ll also be involved in approving applicants for your specific Beer Club. All are welcome to apply!
